The Administration area includes offices for the executive and supporting staff of the library.

Facilities include two (2) Staff Workstations, five (5) Staff Workstations with attached Printers, and two (2) Staff Printers, a (1) Fax machine, and an (1) A/V Unit.

To the South are the Main Stacks, to the East are the Northeast Stacks, and to the West is the Public Services Area.

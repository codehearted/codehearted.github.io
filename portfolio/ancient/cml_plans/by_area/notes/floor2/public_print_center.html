The Public Print Center, located to the east of the Computer Center, on the north west end of the second floor, provides printing services for patrons using the Computer Center, or any other Cliolink terminal. A staff member at InfoStation Three provides assistance to Computer Center and Print Center users.

There are two (2) Color Printers in the Print Center, as well as one (1) Networked Copier, two (2) Public B&W Printers, and a (1) Public Print Queue. Two library card vending machines adorn the east wall (not shown). A single (1) Staff Workstation provides Cliolink services to the staff member on duty at InfoStation Three. Stairs spiral through this area, next to a single (1) "21st Century"-themed Interactive Learning Center.

The Main Stacks are to the southeast. The Systems and Public Services Area are adjacent to the northeast. Stairs lead up to the third floor Terrace and Lobby.

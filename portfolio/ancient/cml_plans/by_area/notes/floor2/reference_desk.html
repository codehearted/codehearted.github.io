The Reference Desk Area includes a row of study rooms along the south wall of the second floor, along with a cluster of eight (8) Public Workstations.

The actual Reference Desk will feature three (3) standard Staff Workstations, one (1) Staff Workstation with a Scanner attached, a (1) Network Copier, one (1) Staff B&W Printer, and a (1) Fax Machine.

North of this area lie the Main Stacks. The Computer Center is to the West.

An escalator provides transportation from this area down to and up from the main Lobby Area on the first floor.

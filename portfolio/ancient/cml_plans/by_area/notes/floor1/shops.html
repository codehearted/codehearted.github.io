The Shops Area consists of a Vending Room, where copying services are available, as well as a machine dispensing temporary library cards to be activated at the circulation desk. There is also a Museum Shop and a Friends Of The Library Store. The Friends' Store has a (1) specialized Staff Workstation to handle register and accounting duties. There is also a single (1) Public Workstation available inside of the Museum Store.

The Shops are located on the south side of the first floor, between the Old World Area and the Express Area. The Circulation Desk is located across the main Lobby from here.

The First Floor Offices house much of the library staff, and are located on the north side of the first floor.

It contains twenty (20) standard Staff Workstations, as well as two (2) Staff Workstations with attached Printers and one (1) Staff Workstation with an attached Scanner. In the copy room, there is one (1) Fax Machine, one (1) B&W Line Printer, one (1) Color Printer, one (1) Networked Copier, and a postage machine (not shown).

There is access to the Circulation Desk to the south, the Children's Area to the west, and the Multimedia and Young Adult areas directly to the east.

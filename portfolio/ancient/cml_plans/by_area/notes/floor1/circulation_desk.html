In the center of the first floor of the library lies the Circulation Desk and the adjacent Book Return Room (included in Circ. Desk Area).

The Circ. Desk has four (4) Staff Workstations each with Checkpoint Readers, as well as two (2) standard Staff Workstations and one (1) Staff B&W Printer. There are two (2) Self-Checkout Stations across from the desk.

The Book Return room has three (3) Checkpoint Readers embedded in the book return slots, books are automatically checked in as they drop ino the pile of materials to be reshelved. A single (1) Staff Workstation is also available in this room.

To the south, across the Lobby, are the Shops. Staff members may go further north into the First Floor Offices.

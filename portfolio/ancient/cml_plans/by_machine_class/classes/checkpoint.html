Checkpoint Materials Security System

The Checkpoint Security System implements physical security or circulating or non-circulating materials by embedding a small metal tag into the material, which will identify itself by a particular serial number to Interrogators positioned at all exits.

Readers

  • Three (3) are integrated into the book returns in the Circulation Desk Area
  • Five (5) are attached to Staff Workstations in the Circulation Desk Area
  • Four (4) are included in the Self-Checkout Stations

Interrogators

  • Located on the first floor at each entrance/exit, including the First Floor Offices (2 Interrogators) and the main Lobby Area (4 Interrogators)
  • Near the southwest elevator on the second and third floors, at the Computer Center (3 Interrogators), and the Terrace and Lobby areas (3 Interrogators), respectively.

Checkpoint Security Monitor

  • There is one (1) Checkpoint Monitor located in the Security Room, part of the main Lobby Area.

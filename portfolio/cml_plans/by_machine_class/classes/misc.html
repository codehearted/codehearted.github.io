Miscellaneous Hardware Notes

Special Area Notes

  • - Third Floor Training Area - Equipment Room

The Training Equipment Room, situated between the two training rooms on level 3, will contain hardware to allow the workstations in the Training Room to connect to the staff network, which is expected to be physically isolated from the network used by the public terminals, or to the network used by the public workstations. The equipment room may contain other specialized hardware for training purposes.

  • - Second Floor Systems Area - Server Room

The Server Room, located in the Systems area on the north side of level 2, will contain many servers and other systems to support the operation of Cliolink 2001 and supporting services. For further information, see the Server Room Hardware section of the Cliolink 2001 Architecture page.

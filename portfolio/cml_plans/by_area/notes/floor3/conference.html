The Conference Room Area is located in approximately the center of the building on the third floor. It includes Conference Rooms 1 and 2, as well as the Green Room above them.

The conference rooms include one (1) A/V Unit each. The Green room has a single (1) Public Workstation with attached Printer.

Adjacent are the Third Floor Terrace and Lobby, and the Staff Lounge Area to the northeast.

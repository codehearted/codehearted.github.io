The Staff Lounge Area includes the Lounge, the Staff Terrace, the Janitorial Closet, the Storage area, and the Kitchen.

It contains a single (1) Staff Workstation with an attached Printer.

The Lounge Area is adjacent to the Third Floor Terrace and Lobby area, and the Conference Room Area.

Stairs on the north side lead down to the Public Services Area on the second floor, then to the Children's Area on the first.

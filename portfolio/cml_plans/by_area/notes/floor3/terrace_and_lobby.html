The Terrace and Lobby Area extends throughout the third floor.

It houses a (1) Staff Workstation and a (1) Black and White Staff Printer near the entrance to the Conference Area, as well as three (3) Interrogators by the southwest elevator.

The Terrace and Lobby area serves as a central hub to the rest of the third floor, including the Conference Area, the Staff Lounge Area, and the Training Area.

This area also connects to the Computer Center on the first floor through an elevator at the southwest corner of the building.

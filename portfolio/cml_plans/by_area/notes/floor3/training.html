Training Rooms 1 and 2, as well as the Equipment room, are included in the Training Room area.

Training Room 1 contains twenty eight (28) Public Workstations, one of which is for the use of the instructor.

Training Room 2 contains one (1) A/V Unit.

The Equipment Room, between them, will have equipment (yet to be determined) for use in controlling the network and computing environment for the Training Rooms.

The Training Room area is surrounded by the Third Floor Terrace and Lobby area.

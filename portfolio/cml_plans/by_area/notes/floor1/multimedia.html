The Multimedia Room provides access to physical materials such as DVDs and CD-ROMs. VHS and audio recordings, as well as other media are also stored in this area. A single (1) Self-Checkout Station is located near the door.

A cluster of four (4) OPAC Stations are located across from the south door.

Located to the south is the Young Adult Area. To the west are the First Floor Offices. Or use the north-south corridor to get to the main Lobby.

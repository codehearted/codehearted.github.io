The Young Adult, or Art Deco Area, located on the far east side of the first floor, is home to InfoStation Two.

Here are located a single (1) Staff Workstation, an (1) Interactive Learning Station, a (1) Public B&W Printer, one (1) Public Print Queue, and eight (8) Public Workstations in a cluster on the east side of the room. Another cluster of four (4) Public Workstations is located across the room on the west side.

This area adjoins the Multimedia Room on it's north side. Directly across the Lobby floor to the south is the Express Area.

An elevator on the west side of the room leads up to the Northeast Stacks on the second level.

The front courtyard is a place to consider for testing a short-range wireless network for patron-owned laptop and handheld computers. Cerritos citizens can enjoy the beautiful weather, cool off by the fountain, all while relaxing and performing their buisness on the internet through Cliolink 2001 and the Cerritos Millennium Library. This network could also be useful for employees of the City of Cerritos, who could also be within the boundaries of the network.

The main entrance on the first floor leads in to the main Lobby Area. An elevator leads to the second floor Computer Center, as well as the Terrace and Lobby on the third floor.

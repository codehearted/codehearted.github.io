The Main Lobby Area includes the lobby and the main entrance, as well as the area surrounding and including InfoStation One, as well as the path to the other first floor areas.

InfoStation One is the first source of answers for the library newcomer, and as such provides three (3) Public Workstations providing all Cliolink 2001 services. A single (1) Staff Workstation provides Cliolink services to staff.

A single (1) A/V Unit (video wall) faces the entrance to the Children's Room, and Five (5) more A/V Units lie juse south of the Circulation Desk Area.

The Lobby Area also includes a small security monitoring room to the left of the main entrance. This room contains one (1) Checkpoint Monitor Station, as well as one (1) standard Staff Workstation, and a (1) Staff B&W Printer.

This area adjoins several high traffic areas, including, to the north, the entrance to the Children's Area. To the south is the Old World Reading Room. Or one can proceed on to the Circulation Desk, Shop Area, Express Area, Young Adult Area, Multimedia Room, or take the stairs up to the Main Stacks or Northeast Stacks. The main entrance leads to the Courtyard and the southwest elevator.

Escalators go between the lobby and the Reference Desk Area on the second level.

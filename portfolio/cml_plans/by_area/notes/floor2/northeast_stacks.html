The Northeast Stacks, located in the northeast corner of the second floor, houses subjects such as biographies, fiction, less recent periodicals, and language books.

This room sports one (1) OPAC Station for public use, as well as four microfiche / microfilm readers with integrated printers (not part of cliolink system thus not shown on map). A single (1) "World Traditions" themed Interactive Learning Center Station is located along the west wall near the entrance.

To the west are the Main Stacks.

There is an elevator going between this area and the Young Adult area on the first floor.

The Public Services area provides office and workspace for city officials on the north edge of the second floor.

It includes five (5) standard Staff Workstations, one (1) Staff Workstation with a Scanner, and two (2) Staff Printers.

Administration lies to the east, Systems to the southeast, and the Computer Center is to the southwest. Stairs lead down to the Children's Area, or up to the Staff Lounge Area on the third floor.

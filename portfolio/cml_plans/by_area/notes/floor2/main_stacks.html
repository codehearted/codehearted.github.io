The Main Stacks occupy the center of the second floor of the library.

There are six (6) Public Workstations in a cluster, as well as one (1) Self-Checkout Station across from the Reference Desk area. There are two (2) OPAC Stations on the far east edge next to the door to the Northeast Stacks.

To the south is the Reference Desk Area. The Public Print Center and the Computer Center are to the west. To the east are the Northeast Stacks, and directly north are the second floor offices - including Systems, Public Services, and Administration.
